I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Cambium research team of Inria Paris.

Previously, I obtained my Ph.D. at the University of Pennsylvania under the direction of Steve Zdancewic, and
obtained my Bachelor of Science (B.S.) at Cornell University.
